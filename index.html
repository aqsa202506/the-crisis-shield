🌍 The Crisis Shield

Global Geopolitical Crisis se khud ko bachayein aur sahi jagah invest karke paisa kamayein.

1. Undersea Internet Cables Cut

🚨 Asar (Problem):

Strait of Hormuz/Red Sea me internet cables katne se global internet slow ho jayega, online banking aur cloud apps ruk jayenge.

📉 Share Market Par Asar:

IT companies (TCS, Infosys) aur saare Tech stocks buri tarah girenge.

💡 Safe Rehne & Kamane ka Tarika:

Paas me kuch cash maintain karein. Paisa kamane ke liye Cybersecurity aur Satellite Internet (jaise Starlink se judi companies) ke stocks me invest karein.

2. Strait of Hormuz Blockade

🚨 Asar (Problem):

Duniya ka 20% crude oil supply ruk jayega. Petrol, Diesel aur Gas ke daam achanak bohot zyada badh jayenge.

📉 Share Market Par Asar:

Airlines, Paint companies, aur Automobile (gaadi banane wali) companies ke shares crash honge.

💡 Safe Rehne & Kamane ka Tarika:

Ghar par solar panel lagayein aur EV ka use karein. Munafe ke liye Renewable Energy (Solar/Wind) aur Oil Exploration stocks khareedein.

3. China-Taiwan Conflict

🚨 Asar (Problem):

Taiwan par hamla hua toh duniya ki 90% advanced microchips ki supply band ho jayegi. Mobile, Laptop aur Tech gadgets behad mehenge ho jayenge.

📉 Share Market Par Asar:

Apple, Nvidia aur electronics banane wali badi companies ke shares girenge.

💡 Safe Rehne & Kamane ka Tarika:

Apne maujuda electronics ko sambhal kar rakhein. Kamane ke liye un deshon ki local Semiconductor Companies (jaise India me Tata) me invest karein jo khud chip bana rahi hain.

4. Global Inflation & Food Crisis

🚨 Asar (Problem):

Ladaai ki wajah se khane-peene ki cheezein mehengi hongi aur cash (Rupee/Dollar) ki value kam hone lagegi.

📉 Share Market Par Asar:

Poora stock market me panic selling hogi aur sabhi index niche aayenge.

💡 Safe Rehne & Kamane ka Tarika:

Ghar me 2-3 mahine ka rashan zaroor rakhein. Apne paise ko dubne se bachane aur munafe ke liye Gold (Sona) aur Silver (Chandi) me invest karein, kyunki crisis me inke daam asmaan chhuenge.
